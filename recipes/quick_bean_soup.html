Quick bean soup

One of my tasty recipes.

About this recipe

This is a reasonably easy and healthy recipe that my mother gave to me.

Ingredients

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 stalks celery, finely sliced
  • 2 cans beans
  • 1 can tomato (chopped or crushed)
  • Vegetable stock powder
  • 3 cloves garlic

Method

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add beans, tomato, celery, 1 or 2 cups water, stock powder.
  3. Heat through. Serve with pepper and nutritional yeast.
